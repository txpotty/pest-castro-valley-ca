Identifying Common Spider Species

In the Castro Valley area, we frequently encounter several types of spiders. Understanding which species are present on your property helps us determine the most effective course of action. Some common spiders we identify include:

  • House Spiders: These are the most common spiders found indoors. They typically build irregular, funnel-shaped webs and are generally harmless
  • Black Widow Spiders: Easily recognizable by the red hourglass shape on their abdomen, these are venomous and require careful handling.
  • Jumping Spiders: Known for their excellent eyesight and ability to jump. They don't build traditional webs for catching prey but may create silk retreats.
  • Wolf Spiders: Larger, hairy spiders that hunt on the ground instead of using webs. They are often mistaken for tarantulas.
  • Orb-Weaver Spiders: These spiders are known for constructing the classic circular webs often seen in gardens and eaves.

Accurate identification is paramount for both effective treatment and ensuring the safety of our technicians and our clients.
